<p><p>The small molecule targeted cancer therapy market comprises three key types: monoclonal antibodies, small molecules, and small molecule drug conjugates. Monoclonal antibodies are engineered to specifically bind cancer cells, aiding in immune response. Small molecules, often low molecular weight compounds, work by interfering with cellular processes to inhibit tumor growth. Small molecule drug conjugates combine the precision of small molecules with the targeting ability of antibodies, delivering potent drugs directly to cancer cells while minimizing effects on healthy tissue.</p></p>
