<p><p>The Dynamic Disturbance Recorder market consists of two main types: Continuous Dynamic Disturbance Recorders and Triggered Dynamic Disturbance Recorders. Continuous recorders continuously capture data on electrical disturbances, providing real-time insights and facilitating preventive measures. In contrast, triggered recorders activate in response to specific events or thresholds, capturing relevant data during disturbances. Both types are essential for monitoring electrical system stability, helping utilities and industries diagnose issues, enhance reliability, and improve overall grid management.</p></p>
