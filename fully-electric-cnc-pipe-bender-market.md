<p><p>The fully electric CNC pipe bender market is segmented primarily into two types: single-head and twin-head. Single-head benders are designed for precision bending of one pipe at a time, making them ideal for smaller production runs or specialized applications. In contrast, twin-head benders can operate two bending units simultaneously, allowing for increased efficiency and productivity in high-volume manufacturing environments. Both types leverage electric technology for improved control, energy efficiency, and reduced maintenance compared to hydraulic models.</p></p>
