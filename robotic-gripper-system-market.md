<p><p>The robotic gripper system market is primarily divided into two types: electric grippers and pneumatic grippers. Electric grippers utilize motors and sensors for precise control and are known for their responsiveness and flexibility, making them ideal for delicate tasks. In contrast, pneumatic grippers rely on compressed air for actuation, offering high gripping force and speed, suited for heavy-duty applications. Both types are vital in automation, enhancing productivity and efficiency across various industries, including manufacturing, logistics, and assembly.</p></p>
