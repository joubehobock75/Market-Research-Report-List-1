<p><p>Casein is a family of related proteins found in milk, making up about 80% of the total protein content in bovine milk. Caseinates are the salts of casein, produced by neutralizing casein with an alkaline substance. These proteins are prized in the food industry for their emulsifying, gelling, and thickening properties, making them suitable for a variety of applications such as dairy products, nutrition supplements, and processed foods.</p><p>The Casein and Caseinates Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.00% during the forecast period, driven by increasing consumer demand for protein-rich foods and the rising popularity of dietary supplements.
