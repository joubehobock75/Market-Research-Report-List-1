<p><strong>The Pylon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tension Tower</li><li>Straight Tower</li><li>Corner Tower</li><li>Transposition Tower</li><li>Terminal Tower</li><li>Spanning Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pylon market types include various structures essential for supporting overhead power lines. Tension towers manage cable tension, ensuring stability across long spans. Straight towers maintain a linear alignment for direct routes, while corner towers facilitate directional changes in transmission lines. Transposition towers are crucial for balancing electrical phases, enhancing system efficiency. Terminal towers signify the endpoints of transmission networks, linking to substations. Spanning towers are designed to cover large gaps, accommodating obstacles like rivers or roads while ensuring safe and reliable power transmission.</p></p>
