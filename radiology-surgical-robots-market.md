<p><p>The radiology surgical robots market can be categorized into three main types: X-ray based, gamma-ray based, and proton-beam based systems. X-ray based robots are commonly used for imaging and guiding minimally invasive procedures. Gamma-ray based systems utilize radioactive tracers for enhanced imaging accuracy in diagnostics and treatment. Proton-beam based technologies employ protons for precise radiation delivery, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. Each type offers unique advantages in the precision and effectiveness of radiological interventions and surgical procedures.</p></p>
