<p><p>The anti-bedsore mattress market is categorized into two main types: static and dynamic. Static anti-bedsore mattresses are designed with high-density foam to evenly distribute body weight, minimizing pressure on susceptible areas. Dynamic anti-bedsore mattresses, on the other hand, incorporate air-filled cells that regularly inflate and deflate, actively shifting pressure points to enhance blood flow and reduce the risk of sores. Both mattress types aim to enhance patient comfort and prevent skin breakdown in those with limited mobility.</p></p>
