<p><ul><li>Threaded Swing Check Valves</li><li>Threaded Ball Check Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threaded check valves are crucial components in fluid systems, preventing backflow while allowing forward flow. The market includes threaded swing check valves, which operate with a hinged disc that swings open and closed, ideal for low-pressure applications. On the other hand, threaded ball check valves utilize a spherical ball that moves to block reverse flow, offering higher sealing effectiveness and durability. Both types are essential in various industries, including water treatment, oil and gas, and HVAC systems, ensuring efficient fluid management.</p></p>
