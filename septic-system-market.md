<p><p>The septic system market includes various types tailored for different soil conditions and wastewater management needs. Conventional Gravity Septic Systems use natural gravitational flow for effluent distribution, while Conventional-Pump Systems employ a pump for enhanced distribution. Pressure Manifold Systems utilize a pressure distribution network to equalize effluent flow. Low-Pressure Pipe (LPP) Systems manage wastewater with a low-pressure system, and Drip Disposal Systems provide precise, slow application of effluent. Other emerging technologies also cater to specific site requirements and environmental concerns.</p></p>
