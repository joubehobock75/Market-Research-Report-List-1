<p><p>The Flame Retardant Polyamide market is primarily categorized into two types: Halogen Type and Halogen-Free Type. Halogen Type polyamides contain halogen elements, such as chlorine or bromine, which effectively inhibit flame spread but may raise environmental and health concerns. In contrast, Halogen-Free Type polyamides utilize alternative additives to achieve flame resistance while being more environmentally friendly and safer for human health. This shift reflects growing demand for sustainable materials across various industries, including automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
