<p><strong>The Karl Fischer Titration Reagent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Reagent</li><li>Coulometric Reagent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Karl Fischer Titration Reagent market is divided into two main types: volumetric and coulometric reagents. Volumetric reagents are used for samples with higher water content, providing direct titration by measuring the volume of reagent required to reach a specific endpoint. In contrast, coulometric reagents are suited for samples with lower water content, generating iodine electrochemically to react with the sample in a closed system. Both types are crucial for accurate moisture analysis in a range of industries.</p></p>
