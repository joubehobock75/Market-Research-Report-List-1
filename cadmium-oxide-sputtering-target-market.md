<p><p>Cadmium oxide sputtering targets are classified into two main types: rotary and non-rotatable. Rotary targets are cylindrical and allow for continuous material deposition, enhancing uniformity in thin films. They are ideal for large-scale applications due to their efficiency and longer lifespan. Non-rotatable targets, being flat, are typically used for smaller projects or specialized applications where precision is crucial. Both types cater to diverse industries, including electronics and photovoltaics, depending on specific deposition requirements and production scales.</p></p>
