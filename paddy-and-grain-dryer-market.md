<p><p>The Paddy and Grain Dryer market comprises two primary types: stationary and mobile dryers. </p><p>Stationary dryers are fixed installations designed for large-scale operations, often used in commercial settings. They provide high drying capacities and are efficient for bulk processing. </p><p>In contrast, mobile dryers are portable units that can be relocated easily, making them ideal for farmers with smaller operations or variable harvesting conditions. They offer flexibility and convenience for drying grains directly at the harvest site, enhancing efficiency and minimizing post-harvest losses.</p></p>
