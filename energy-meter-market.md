<p><ul><li>Electromechanical Meters</li><li>Electronic Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The energy meter market can be categorized into two main types: electromechanical meters and electronic meters. Electromechanical meters, typically utilizing rotating disks and mechanical components, measure energy based on electromagnetic induction. They are widely used for their durability but are less accurate and less efficient compared to their modern counterparts. Electronic meters, on the other hand, use digital technology and microprocessors to provide more precise readings, advanced functionalities, and communication capabilities, making them suitable for smart grid applications and energy management.</p></p>
