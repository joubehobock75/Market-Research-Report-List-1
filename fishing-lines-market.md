<p><p>The fishing lines market is primarily segmented into two types: braided and monofilament. Braided lines consist of multiple strands woven together, offering high strength, minimal stretch, and excellent sensitivity, making them ideal for heavy-duty fishing. Monofilament lines are made from a single strand of nylon, providing good flexibility and ease of handling, suited for various fishing techniques. Each type caters to different fishing environments and angler preferences, influencing their choice based on factors like visibility, durability, and casting distance.</p></p>
