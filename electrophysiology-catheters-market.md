<p><p>The electrophysiology catheters market is divided into two main types: monitoring devices and treatment devices. Electrophysiology monitoring devices are used to diagnose and assess cardiac arrhythmias by recording electrical activity within the heart. These include catheters equipped with sensors and mapping systems. In contrast, electrophysiology treatment devices focus on therapeutic interventions, such as ablation catheters that destroy abnormal tissue causing arrhythmias, offering patients a targeted approach to restore normal heart rhythms. Both segments play a crucial role in managing cardiac conditions.</p></p>
