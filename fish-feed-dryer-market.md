<p><ul><li>Sunken Type</li><li>Floating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fish feed dryer market comprises two main types: sunken type and floating type. Sunken type dryers are designed for feed that sinks in water, commonly used for species like catfish and tilapia. They ensure efficient moisture removal while maintaining nutrient quality. Floating type dryers, on the other hand, cater to feed that remains buoyant, suitable for fish like trout and carp. These dryers enhance feed pellet durability and promote optimal feeding behavior, catering to specific aquaculture needs.</p></p>
