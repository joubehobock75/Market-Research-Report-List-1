<p><ul><li>Sugar Coating</li><li>Organic Film Coating</li><li>Aqueous Film Coating</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet coating systems enhance the appearance, stability, and controlled release of pharmaceuticals. Sugar coating involves layering sucrose and other ingredients for a glossy finish, while organic film coating uses synthetic polymers to form a thin, protective layer. Aqueous film coating utilizes water-based solutions, providing faster drying times and reduced solvent use. Other coating methods may include enteric coatings that resist stomach acid or targeting specific intestinal release. Each type serves specific drug delivery and regulatory needs within the market.</p></p>
