<p><p>The Board Level EMI Shields Market is categorized into one-piece and two-piece shields. One-piece board level shields consist of a single, integrated metal enclosure that provides complete electromagnetic interference (EMI) protection for sensitive components. Conversely, two-piece board level shields comprise two separate sections that can be adjusted or removed, offering flexibility in design and accessibility for maintenance. Both types aim to minimize EMI, ensuring optimal performance and reliability of electronic devices across various industries.</p></p>
