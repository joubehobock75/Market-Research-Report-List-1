<p><p>Portland limestone cements (PLC) are classified into two main market types based on consistency: standard consistency and extra thick consistency. Standard consistency PLC is designed for general construction purposes, providing a balance of workability and strength. In contrast, extra thick consistency PLC offers enhanced durability and lower permeability, making it ideal for specialized applications in challenging environments. Both types cater to diverse construction needs while promoting sustainability by incorporating limestone, reducing the carbon footprint of traditional cement.</p></p>
