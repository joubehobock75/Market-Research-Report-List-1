<p><p>The construction paints and coatings market comprises two primary types: water-based and solvent-based paints. Water-based paints utilize water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions, easier cleanup, and quick drying times, making them environmentally friendly and user-friendly. In contrast, solvent-based paints use organic solvents, providing superior durability and resistance to harsh weather conditions, making them ideal for exterior applications. Each type has distinct advantages that cater to different project requirements and environmental considerations in the construction industry.</p></p>
