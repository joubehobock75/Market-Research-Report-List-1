<p><p>The Emergency Notification System (ENS) market is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises systems. Cloud-based ENS solutions offer flexibility and scalability, allowing organizations to quickly deploy notifications from anywhere with internet access. They typically require lower upfront investment and facilitate automatic updates. Each type serves different organizational needs based on size, budget, and security priorities.</p></p>
