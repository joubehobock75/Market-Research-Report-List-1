<p><p>The zirconia-based porcelain veneer market comprises two primary types: no-layering porcelain and layering with porcelain. No-layering porcelain veneers are pre-fabricated, offering a solid, uniform structure that eliminates the need for additional layering, enhancing durability and reducing fabrication time. In contrast, layering with porcelain involves applying multiple layers of material to achieve a more lifelike appearance, allowing for greater customization in aesthetics. Both types cater to different preferences in appearance and functional needs, appealing to diverse customer segments within dental aesthetics.</p></p>
