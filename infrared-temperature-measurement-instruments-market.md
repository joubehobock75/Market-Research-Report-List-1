<p><strong>The Infrared Temperature Measurement Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Temperature Measurement Instruments</li><li>Non-Contact Temperature Measurement Instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared temperature measurement instruments market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact temperature measurement instruments. </p><p>Contact temperature measurement instruments involve physical contact with the object, utilizing sensors that measure thermal conductivity. They provide accurate readings but may not be suitable for moving or hazardous objects. </p><p>In contrast, non-contact temperature measurement instruments utilize infrared technology to measure the thermal radiation emitted by an object from a distance, allowing for quick and safe measurements in various environments. Both types cater to different industrial and residential applications.</p></p>
