<p><p>The refrigerant oils market is primarily categorized into mineral oils and synthetic oils. Mineral oils, derived from refining crude oil, are commonly used in traditional refrigerants, offering cost-effectiveness but limited thermal stability. Synthetic oils, on the other hand, are chemically engineered to provide enhanced performance, thermal stability, and improved compatibility with newer refrigerants, particularly in high-efficiency systems. The choice between these oils depends on application requirements, operational conditions, and regulatory compliance for environmental sustainability.</p></p>
