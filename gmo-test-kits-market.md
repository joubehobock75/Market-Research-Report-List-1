<p><strong>The GMO Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR</li><li>ELISA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GMO test kits market is primarily categorized into two types: PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) and ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay). PCR kits detect genetically modified organisms by amplifying specific DNA sequences, offering high sensitivity and specificity, making them ideal for various samples. In contrast, ELISA kits identify GMOs by measuring proteins associated with genetic modifications, typically providing quicker results and ease of use. Both methods play crucial roles in ensuring food safety and regulatory compliance in the agri-food sector.</p></p>
