<p><strong>The Medical Grade Microneedle Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hollow Microneedle</li><li>Solid Microneedle</li><li>Dissolving Microneedle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical grade microneedle devices market is categorized into three main types: hollow microneedles, solid microneedles, and dissolving microneedles. Hollow microneedles allow fluid delivery for vaccines or medications through a tube structure. Solid microneedles, lacking a lumen, primarily serve for skin penetration and can enhance transdermal drug absorption. Dissolving microneedles are designed to dissolve after insertion, releasing the drug directly into the skin, offering an efficient and minimally invasive delivery method for pharmaceuticals and vaccines.</p></p>
