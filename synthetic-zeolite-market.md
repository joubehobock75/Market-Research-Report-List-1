<p><p>The synthetic zeolite market includes different types based on their structures and applications. A-type zeolite, known for its high adsorption capacity, is commonly used in detergents and water purification. X-type zeolite offers larger pore sizes, making it suitable for catalytic processes in the petroleum industry. Y-type zeolite, with excellent ion-exchange properties, is favored in refining and petrochemical applications. Additionally, other zeolite types, such as ZSM-5, serve specialized roles in various industrial and environmental applications, enhancing overall market diversity.</p></p>
