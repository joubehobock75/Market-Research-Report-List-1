<p><ul><li>Circular Stapler</li><li>Linear Stapler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suture machinery market includes two primary types: circular staplers and linear staplers. Circular staplers are used for creating anastomoses in gastrointestinal surgeries, allowing for efficient circular suturing. They are ideal for procedures requiring a round closure. Linear staplers, on the other hand, are designed for straight line suturing, commonly utilized in various surgical specialties, including thoracic and abdominal surgeries. Both types enhance surgical precision and efficiency, significantly improving patient outcomes during minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
