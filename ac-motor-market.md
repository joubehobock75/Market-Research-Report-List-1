<p><ul><li>Synchronous Motors</li><li>Induction Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC motor market is primarily divided into synchronous and induction motors. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed, synchronized with the supply frequency, offering high efficiency and precision, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent speed. Induction motors, more commonly used, rely on electromagnetic induction for operation and are favored for their simplicity, robustness, and cost-effectiveness. Together, these motor types cater to various industries, including manufacturing, HVAC, and transportation, driving advancements in automation and energy efficiency.</p></p>
