<p><strong>The Polyimide-based Flexible Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyimide flexible heater (no backing)</li><li>Aluminum / Copper Plated Polyimide Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide-based flexible heater market comprises two main types: polyimide flexible heaters without backing and aluminum/copper plated polyimide heaters. Polyimide flexible heaters (no backing) are lightweight and can be easily shaped, making them ideal for various applications. In contrast, aluminum/copper plated polyimide heaters integrate metal layers to enhance thermal conductivity and durability, providing better heat distribution. Both types cater to industries like aerospace, automotive, and electronics, where efficient and space-saving heating solutions are essential.</p></p>
