<p><p>Chiller systems are essential for cooling applications across various industries and can be categorized into several types. Centrifugal chillers utilize a rotating mechanism to compress refrigerant, ideal for large-scale cooling needs. Scroll chillers use spiral elements for efficient cooling in moderate capacities. Screw chillers employ two interlocking screws for enhanced efficiency in commercial settings. Other types include absorption chillers, thermoelectric chillers, and cable chillers, each serving specific applications and offering diverse operational benefits.</p></p>
