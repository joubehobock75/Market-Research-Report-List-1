<p><p>The Low Voltage Reactor market comprises two primary types: dry-type reactors and oil-immersed reactors. Dry-type reactors utilize air for cooling, making them suitable for environments sensitive to oil spills, offering reduced maintenance and enhanced safety. In contrast, oil-immersed reactors utilize insulating oil for cooling and provide improved heat dissipation and efficiency, often preferred in industrial applications. Both types serve critical roles in managing voltage levels and enhancing power quality across various sectors, including utilities and manufacturing.</p></p>
