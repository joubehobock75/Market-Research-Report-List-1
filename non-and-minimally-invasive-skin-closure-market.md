<p><p>The Non and Minimally Invasive Skin Closure market primarily encompasses two main types: adhesive-based and micro anchor-based systems. Adhesive-based closures utilize medical-grade adhesives to bond skin edges together, promoting secure wound healing without sutures. Micro anchor-based systems employ tiny anchors that gently grasp the skin, providing a reliable closure while minimizing tissue trauma. Both methods aim to reduce scarring, enhance patient comfort, and accelerate healing times compared to traditional suturing techniques, making them increasingly popular in various medical procedures.</p></p>
