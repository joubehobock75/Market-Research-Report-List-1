<p><p>The wall charger market is categorized primarily into three types: single-port chargers, multi-port chargers, and wire chargers. Single-port chargers offer basic functionality for charging one device at a time, making them compact and portable. Multi-port chargers cater to users with multiple devices, allowing simultaneous charging, often with varying power outputs. Wire chargers, on the other hand, include an integrated cable, providing a convenient all-in-one solution. Each type targets specific consumer needs, emphasizing convenience, efficiency, and versatility.</p></p>
