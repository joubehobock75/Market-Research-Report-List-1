<p><strong>The VOC Testing Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC Testing Device market primarily includes two types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID devices utilize ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, enabling precise and real-time detection of low concentrations of VOCs, making them ideal for environmental monitoring. In contrast, MOS sensors detect VOCs through changes in electrical resistance caused by gas adsorption on metal-oxide surfaces, offering a cost-effective solution for various applications, including indoor air quality monitoring and industrial safety.</p></p>
