<p><p>The textile scouring agent market is primarily divided into two types: non-ionic and anionic agents. Non-ionic scouring agents are typically used for their mildness and effectiveness in removing impurities without affecting the fabric’s properties, making them suitable for delicate textiles. Anionic scouring agents, on the other hand, are more aggressive in removing dirt and greases, providing strong cleaning power, which makes them ideal for heavier fabrics. The choice between these agents depends on fabric type and desired cleaning intensity.</p></p>
